Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Chicha AI ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our AI-powered food ordering assistant.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our application, interact with our AI agent, or connect third-party services like Uber Eats.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Identity Information: When you sign in via Microsoft or GitHub, we collect your basic profile information (name, email address) to create your user account.
  • Chat Data: We collect the text messages you send to Chicha AI to process your requests and improve our agent's responses.
  • Location Data: With your explicit permission, we collect your geolocation (latitude/longitude) to find restaurants and estimate delivery times near you.

B. Third-Party Integrations (Uber Eats)

When you choose to connect your Uber account to Chicha AI, we access specific information via the Uber API based on the permissions you grant:

  • Profile Information: To identify your account context.
  • Delivery Addresses: To help you select where to send your order.
  • Order History: To answer questions about your past meals or status.

Note: We do not store your Uber password. Authentication is handled securely via OAuth 2.0 tokens.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To find restaurants, build carts, and place orders on your behalf.
  • AI Personalization: To remember your dietary preferences (e.g., "No pickles", "Vegan") during your session.
  • Communication: To send you real-time updates about your order status (e.g., "Order Confirmed", "Rider Assigned").

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We transmit your order details to Uber Eats or Contoso Burgers to fulfill your request.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption of data in transit and at rest. Your OAuth tokens for third-party services are stored securely in our database and are never exposed to the client-side browser.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data (including chat history and linked accounts).
  • Disconnect third-party integrations (Uber) at any time via your User Profile card.
